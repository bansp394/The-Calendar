Dial Picker

Medieval Era

The dial picker allows users to select a date by rotating a circular interface, similar to turning a knob. As the dial spins, months, days, or years move along the circular path and align at a fixed selection point. The user stops the rotation when the desired value reaches the center or indicator. This creates a smooth and intuitive interaction that feels tactile and continuous.
