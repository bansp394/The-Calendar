Magnetic Picker

Connected Era

The Magnetic Date Picker introduces a more fluid and intuitive way of selecting a date by using proximity-based interaction instead of traditional scrolling or tapping. As the user moves their cursor or finger across the interface, nearby dates are subtly attracted, increasing in size and brightness to indicate focus. This creates a dynamic sense of movement, where the interface responds to the user’s presence rather than fixed input. The interaction feels exploratory and organic, transforming date selection into a more engaging and responsive experience.
